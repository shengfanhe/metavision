Metavision is a startup trying to streamline the production of 3D objects at scale. In that way, we can mirror our physical world for any industrial scenarios to design, simulate and control.

About the team


Our team is made up with the engineers and technical artist from the top companies such as Netease Games, Bytedance, and Tencent. We have established strategic partnership with Unity Technologies China, and leveraged the most advanced 3D engine to build industry solutions for various clients.



We are headquartered in Hangzhou, China, by the Qiantang River. At Metavision, we believe in putting people at the center of everything we do. Our community of designers is focused on helping our clients and partners—at the individual, organizational, and societal level—build their creative capacity and their ability to grow, adapt, and routinely innovate.
